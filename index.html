About Me

I like to build things with like minded people.

After realizing that physical therapy was not the correct career path for me I found my way back to software development. I went through various online resources and a few classes at my local college before I realized the pace at which I was being taught did not challenge me in the way sports previously had. My research into ways to speed up my development growth then led me to LearningFuze, a Full Immersion Web Development Program in Orange County. The course taught me invaluable skills to bring to the real world work environment as well as solidifying my ability to pick up new technologies quickly and to hit the ground running.

I love developing applications with or without relying on libraries and frameworks and I understand the pros and cons of using the newest and hottest technologies in a production environment
